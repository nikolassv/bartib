# Contributing to Bartib

First of all, thank you for your interest in contributing to Bartib! I am grateful for the community that has grown around this project.

## A Note from the Author

I created Bartib several years ago to be a simple, focused tool for my personal needs. I'm thrilled that it has become useful to others as well. The feedback and support from the community have been incredibly encouraging.

However, it's important to clarify the project's philosophy moving forward. I consider Bartib to be **"finished" software**. It was designed with a specific, limited scope, and my goal is to maintain its simplicity and stability rather than to expand it with new functionality.

## What This Means for Contributions

I am still committed to maintaining Bartib and ensuring it remains a reliable tool. Therefore, I will gladly review and consider pull requests that fall into the following categories:

*   **Bug Fixes:** Anything that corrects a demonstrable bug or unexpected behavior.
*   **Maintenance:** Updates to dependencies, build scripts, or other changes required to keep the project healthy and compatible with modern systems.
*   **Fixing Rough Edges:** Minor tweaks that improve the existing user experience without adding new features (e.g., improving error messages, fixing layout issues).

## Feature Requests

While I appreciate the creative ideas and enthusiasm, **I do not intend to integrate new features into Bartib**. This project was never meant to be a feature-rich suite, and I want to keep it true to its original, simple vision.

Therefore, pull requests that introduce new features will, respectfully, not be merged. I hope you can understand this decision to keep the project focused.

Thank you again for your understanding and for being a part of the Bartib community.
